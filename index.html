Composition based oxidation state prediction of materials using deep learning language models
Nihang Fu, Jeffrey Hu, Feng Ying, Gregory Morrison, Hans-Conrad zur Loye, Jianjun Hu
Advanced Science, 10(28), 2301011 ¬∑ 2023
We proposed BERTOS, a novel deep learning-based BERT transformer model, to predict the oxidation states of elements in inorganic compounds using only chemical composition. Our model achieves 96.82% accuracy on the ICSD dataset, enabling large-scale screening for new material discovery where structures are unknown.
